Game Pillars

What

"Survive the madness of Boom Boulevard, a chaotic explosion-filled race and be the last one standing."

How

"Boom Boulevard is set to detonate. Only one survivor gets out before it explodes."

Why

"Roll to advance down the board and play explosive action cards to sabotage, defend, and eliminate your rivals."

Development Context

Boom Boulevard draws heavy inspiration from the high-tension interactive mechanics of Exploding Kittens combined with the chaotic movement elements of Sorry!. Our group's focus was on creating a balanced "risk vs reward" system where leader targeting is natural and every turn feels unpredictable.
